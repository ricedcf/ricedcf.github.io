About Us

Grad Student - Medical Students - Young Professionals

Welcome! We invite you to join the Rice DCF group that was just launched in summer 2015. This is an amazing opportunity for graduate students, medical students, and young professionals to get connected by creating a sense of family and allowing you to build dynamic relationships with others! No matter what your background, interests, goals, or stage of life, Rice DCF will be a great fit for you. Our main goal is to get everyone connected, foster friendships, and grow our relationship with God. This is a small coed group that will meet throughout the semester in different areas around the Houston area including Rice Village, Herman Park, and Montrose. Our meetings will be self-led by group members in order to serve each others' needs by building a strong community through encouragement and support. We invite you to join us and get connected with our community!

Mission Statement

Rice Discernment through Catholic Fellowship (Rice DCF) is a group that ministers to young Catholic adults in their 20’s and 30’s and provides a supportive environment for Catholic fellowship. Rice DCF believes that fellowship is crucial among this age group where life guidance and affirmation of Catholic values is much needed. Rice DCF hopes to strengthen its members' faith foundation in Christ as they journey in their personal discernment.
